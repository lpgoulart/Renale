COMPANY


RENALE intends to present its patients with a differentiated environment, especially in their hemodialysis room. Patients with chronic diseases should be treated in services that are like the extension of their homes, with: safety, comfort, confidence, credibility and, above all, respect.

At RENALE, the priority is the patients and their families, and the search for humanized treatment is a constant in the care of the team. Both the clinical and nursing staff, the multiprofessional team and the support team have experience in the treatment of chronic patients, and training and continuing education activities are constantly carried out with a view to patient safety. Patients who visit (in transit) our beautiful capital (Natal-RN) will also be welcomed as patients of the program, receiving all the attention, which is characteristic of the clinic.


RENALE'S DIFFERENTIALS:

  1. Humanized environment;
  2. Absence of reuse of materials;
  3. Capillaries for single use;
  4. New and modern equipment;
  5. Present and active multidisciplinary team;
  6. Individualized and followed snacks with nutritionist;
  7. Presence of on-call doctors;
  8. Double-step water treatment.

About us
